## Derived $D^\pi(-Q^2)$ band (exact algebra; conservative envelope)

Exact identity used:
$$
D^\pi(-Q^2)=\frac{2\hat\Theta^\pi(-Q^2)-(4+q^2)\,A^\pi(-Q^2)}{3q^2},\qquad q^2\equiv Q^2/m_\pi^2,\ q^2>0.

- $D_{\min}$ uses $\hat\Theta_{\min}$ and $A_{\max}$.
