## B) Primary Physics Justification (arXiv:2412.00848)

In `tranden_fin.tex`, subsection `Elastic region and threshold behavior`:

1) Watson theorem:
$$
f^I_J(s)=|f^I_J(s)|e^{i\delta^I_J(s)}\quad\Rightarrow\quad {\rm Im}f^I_J(s)=|f^I_J(s)|\sin\delta^I_J(s).
$$

2) For attractive phases $0\le\delta\le\pi$, the imaginary part is nonnegative.

3) The paper states that the elastic regime is consistent in practice up to the $K\bar K$ threshold and concludes:
$$
{\rm Im}A(s),\ {\rm Im}F(s),\ {\rm Im}\Theta(s)\ge 0,\qquad 4m_\pi^2\le s\le 4m_K^2.
$$

We treat this as an **optional, auditable physics input**: it is distinct from the transverse-density positivity kernel constraint $A(b)\ge 0$ and from the GTB $(S,\mathcal{F},\rho)$ PSD envelope.
