## Dual certificate at $Q^*$

For the minimization at fixed $Q^*$, the solver diagnostics provide:
- `objective_value`: primal objective (the discretized integral part),
- `dual_objective_value`: solver-reported dual objective for the same conic program.

Since the UV tail contributes a fixed constant `tail_const(Q^*)` added after the solve, we can compute a dual lower bound on $A_{\min}$:
$$
A_{\min}^{\rm dual}(Q^*) = ({\rm dual\_objective\_value}) + {\rm tail\_const}(Q^*),
$$
with `tail_const(Q^*) = A_{\min}(Q^*) - objective_value` taken from the same run output.
