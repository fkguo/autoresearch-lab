## What changed (candidate publishable innovation)

We tightened the pion momentum GFF $A^\pi(-Q^2)$ lower bounds by moving beyond:
- envelope-LP relaxations (pointwise maximize $|A(s)|$ independently), and
- Im-only conic coupling ($\rho(s)\ge ({\rm Im}A(s))^2/c_{\rm fac}(s)$),

to a **dispersion-coupled modulus formulation**:

1) Use a PV dispersion relation to reconstruct the *real part* on the cut from ${\rm Im}A$:
   $$ {\rm Re}A(s_i)= (1-f_0)+\frac{1}{\pi}\,{\rm PV}\!\int dx\,{\rm Im}A(x)\left(\frac{1}{x-s_i}-\frac{1}{x}\right), $$
   discretized on the same $s$-grid (plus an explicit UV tail contribution).

2) Enforce the **full modulus cone** on a subset of grid points:
   $$ ({\rm Re}A(s_i))^2+({\rm Im}A(s_i))^2 \le c_{\rm fac}(s_i)\,\rho(s_i). $$

This couples analyticity + GTB positivity more tightly than earlier relaxations.
