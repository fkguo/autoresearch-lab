## Core Technical Claim to Review

We use the following executable constraint chain:

1) (2412) **Transverse positivity**: interpret $A(b)$ as a nonnegative transverse density for $\Theta^{++}$, hence enforce $A(b)\ge 0$.

2) (2412) **Unsubtracted dispersion**:
$$
A^\pi(-Q^2)=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{4}^{\infty}ds\,\frac{{\rm Im}\,A^\pi(s)}{s+Q^2}.
$$

3) Combine (2) with the Fourier–Bessel transform definition of the transverse density:
$$
A(b)=\int_0^\infty \frac{q\,dq}{2\pi}J_0(bq)\,A^\pi(-q^2)
=\frac{1}{2\pi^2}\int_{4}^{\infty}ds\,{\rm Im}\,A^\pi(s)\,K_0(b\sqrt{s}).
$$
This turns $A(b)\ge 0$ into **linear inequalities on** ${\rm Im}\,A^\pi(s)$ (since $K_0>0$).

4) Add a conservative envelope input (from earlier LP guardrails): $|{\rm Im}\,A(s_i)|\le U(s_i)$, with $U(s)$ imported from:
`runs/2026-02-16-d0-eta-envelope-v1/results.json` profile `eta_floor_0p6`.

5) Solve LPs to bound $A^\pi(-Q^2)$ at multiple $Q^2$ values.
